Our Story


This Isn't a Fairytale - It's Flow, and It Works

In our thirties, we - Kunal and Nira - stepped away from successful corporate careers with financial freedom but our path wasn’t built on avoidance, compromise, or relentless hustle. Even in demanding roles, we chose to live intentionally - immersing ourselves in music and dance, enjoying stillness in nature, exploring cultures, serving the community, and savouring moments of luxury. We consistently made space for what made us feel alive.

And as we lived this way, we noticed life unfolding with magical ease. Doors opened. Rules bent. Challenges softened. The faintest intentions drew the right resources and opportunities. Over time, we realised: perhaps nothing is truly “impossible”or “unlikely.”

This flow, we discovered, stemmed from two deep alignments - our partnership with each other and our connection to life itself.

Nira, rooted in being, cultivated mastery over the inner economy - accessing an intelligence more accurate than the mind.

Kunal, grounded in doing, mastered the outer economy - finance, communication, strategy, leadership - navigating these complexities with ease and structure.

By leaning into each other’s strengths, we learnt how to move effortlessly between the two dimensions of life: the human and the being. In this dance, we discovered a sweet spot—where stillness propels clear movement, strategic action and playful surrender.

This isn’t luck - it’s available to anyone ready to turn inward and embrace both dimensions of life.

The Consultants

Nira
From Dispute Resolution to Dispute Dissolution

Are you yearning for greater clarity, confidence, peace - or simply a spring in your step? I help people create lives rooted in ease and alignment, where success and fulfillment arise naturally.

I blend structure and firsthand experience from a high-pressure legal career with a lifelong dedication to self-enquiry. Triple-qualified as a lawyer in England & Wales, Australia, and Malaysia, I left the law to follow my truest calling: helping others live more effortless and fulfilling lives.

My own transformation began with severe anxiety, body image struggles, and a persistent sense of emptiness. Over 15 years of disciplined inner work towards self-mastery, exploring consciousness, and human behaviour studies revealed that: dissolving inner conflicts is far more liberating than resolving them. By deepening my experience of myself - beyond thoughts and emotions—I met a greater intelligence at play, and found I could accomplish far more by being than by doing. This is the essence of my methods.

My approach is led by compassionate presence and clear intuitive insight. Clients often describe our sessions as light-hearted yet powerfully transformative - like speaking to an old friend who sees you in the deepest sense. I don’t impose “positive thinking” or offer surface-level fixes. Instead, I hold a space where insecurities soften, stories lose their grip, and answers emerge from within. See Testimonials for client anecdotes.

With expertise in how the mind operates and an unfailing method of inquiry, I guide clients to tune into their highest intelligence and take fearless action toward the life they truly want.

I speak to the part of you that’s done pretending, and quietly ready to live with more clarity and freedom.

Kunal
From Leftover Fries to Financial Freedom

Are you tired of feeling stuck between where you are and where you truly want to be? Do your biggest goals in your career, finances, or personal life feel out of reach?

I help bridge that gap. We’ll turn your aspirations into a clear, practical roadmap, and walk that path together.

Growing up in Hong Kong, one of the world's most expensive cities, I survived on US$1.30 a day, often eating leftover fries from schoolmates. My Chinese name (柏冠拿), given by an immigration officer, translates to “grab the championship” - a phrase that became my life’s blueprint.

With three younger brothers and a single working mother, I decided to become fully self-reliant at 14, tutoring alongside my studies so my family could stay afloat.

After leaving a promising legal education to pursue business management, I succeeded at conferences and competitions and even founded a biotech company. I eventually chose a 15-year corporate career, where I scaled the ranks at HSBC and Amazon, becoming a Deputy Global Head at Amazon in under 12 years of working.

Looking back, every decision made was in tune with an inner knowing, choosing not to follow convention, yet still finding success.

By 35, I had reached financial freedom. While the corporate roles and accolades brought prominence, my deepest fulfilment always came from mentoring others. I realised my true mission: to dedicate my life to enabling others “grab the championship” in their own lives.

Together, we will design and walk a path that is authentically yours - helping you thrive in your career, master your finances and develop the life skills to flourish.
